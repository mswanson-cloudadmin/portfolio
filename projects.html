Engagement Score

Situation: Inability to track engagement, which slowed outreach and caused missed opportunities.
Task: Create a solution to boost engagement tracking and outreach efficiency.
Action: Built a real-time Salesforce-based model with a 20-point scoring system, reports and dashboards, and trained the team.
Result: Increased member retention by 20%, enhanced staff outreach confidence, and streamlined marketing.

Titan Sign

Situation: Manual e-signature processes in Salesforce were time-consuming, lacked bulk sending, struggled with multi-signer workflows, and had no real-time visibility.
Task: Implement a solution for bulk sending, auto-updates, dynamic workflows, and tracking.
Action: Configured a “Titan Sign” button, screen flow, web form, and tracking integration.
Result: Reduced staff workload by 60%, cut turnaround time by 20%, ensured 100% accuracy, and lifted morale.
